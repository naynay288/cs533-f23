Favorite recent TV show: The Bear

The Bear is a Hulu original show about a world-class chef and his efforts to save his family's restaurant after the death of his brother.
I loved pretty much every performance from the main and supporting cast. That and the writing definitely makes the characters feel like real and relatable people.
The show also has amazing cinematography. One episode (Season 1 Episode 7) was shot entirely in one continuous take and it's one of the most impressive things I've ever seen.
