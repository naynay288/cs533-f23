Parasite

Parasite is a South Korean film by director Bong Joon-ho that blends some of my favorite genres in movies like thriller, suspense, drama, and a little bit of heist/mystery. At first watch it is a thrilling family drama that turns deadly but can really open your mind to the state of poverty, classism, and social-economic structures within the world. I genuinely wish I could watch it for the first time as it was such a riveting experience like no other.

P.S. I recommend all of his other movies such as Snowpiercer, Memories of Murder, and The Host.
